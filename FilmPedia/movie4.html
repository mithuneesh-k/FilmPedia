Story

Four misfits are transported to the Minecraft world, a blocky realm where they must learn to survive and thrive. Using the power of creativity and teamwork, they embark on an epic quest to find their way home while battling hostile mobs and uncovering the secrets of this digital universe. Along the way, they discover the importance of friendship and imagination.
