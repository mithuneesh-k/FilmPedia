Story

Spanning 6,000 years, the story begins at the end of the Kurukshetra War and leaps into the dystopian year 2898 AD. In the last city of Kashi, the elite live in the floating "Complex" while the poor suffer below. When Sumathi, a lab subject, escapes with a divine child in her womb, she is protected by the immortal Ashwatthama. Bhairava, a selfish bounty hunter, is caught in the crossfire, leading to a clash of mythic proportions that bridges the gap between the Mahabharata and the future.

Reviews

  • Visual Mastery: Praised for its "Indofuturist" design, blending Indian temple architecture with cyberpunk technology.
  • Amitabh's Performance: Critics and fans unanimously called Amitabh Bachchan's portrayal of Ashwatthama the "soul of the film."
  • IMDb: Currently holds a strong 7.6/10, ranking as one of the most popular Indian films of 2024.
  • The Climax: The final 20 minutes are widely considered a landmark in Indian cinema for their epic scale and reveals.

Watcher's Guide

  • Runtime: 181 minutes (3 hours 1 min).
  • Cinematic Universe: This is Part 1 of the Kalki Cinematic Universe (KCU).
  • Best Format: Recommended in IMAX 3D for the full scale of the "Complex" and battle sequences.
  • Language: Originally shot in Telugu and Hindi; available in multiple languages.

Fun Facts

  • It is the most expensive Indian film ever made, with a budget of ₹600 crore.
  • The custom-built supercar "Bujji" is a real, functioning 6-ton vehicle designed and manufactured specifically for the film.
  • The film features major cameos from stars like Dulquer Salmaan, Vijay Deverakonda, and Mrunal Thakur.
  • Director Nag Ashwin took 5 years to complete the world-building and pre-production for this project.
