Story

Sathya is a struggling cartoonist living in a slum who believes in 'adjusting' to injustice rather than fighting it. When his family is relocated to a poorly constructed government apartment, his life takes a surreal turn after a near-death experience. He begins hearing a god-like voice that dictates his every move like a panel in a comic book. Guided by this voice, the cowardly Sathya is transformed into a reluctant savior of the people, eventually facing off against the ruthless Minister Jeyakodi.

Reviews

  • The Hindu: Praised as a "fascinating superhero film" that respects commercial sensibilities while offering sharp social commentary.
  • IMDb: Holds a solid 7.4/10, with audiences celebrating the unique concept and Sivakarthikeyan's transition from coward to hero.
  • Technical: The "voice" gimmick (provided by Vijay Sethupathi) was hailed as a brilliant narrative tool that added both humor and tension.
  • Action: The fight sequences were noted for their creative choreography, where the hero moves as if being 'pulled' by invisible strings.

Watcher's Guide

  • Release Date: July 14, 2023
  • Runtime: 163 minutes
  • Genre Mix: It's a rare blend of political satire and fantasy-superhero elements.
  • Telugu Version: The film was released in Telugu under the title Mahaveerudu, with actor Ravi Teja providing the voiceover.

Fun Facts

  • The film's title is a tribute to the 1986 classic Maaveeran starring Rajinikanth.
  • Vijay Sethupathi recorded his voiceover for the entire film in just a few days, but his presence is felt throughout the runtime.
  • Director Madonne Ashwin previously won two National Awards for his debut film, Mandela.
  • The comic book panels seen in the film were specially designed to mirror the actual cinematography of the action scenes.
