Story

With his identity now revealed as Spider-Man, Peter Parker seeks Doctor Strange's help to make the world forget. However, the spell goes wrong, tearing open the multiverse and bringing dangerous villains from alternate dimensions. Peter must find a way to save both his world and the villains themselves, learning that with great power comes great responsibility.

Reviews

  • IMDb: With an 8.2/10 rating and over 1 million votes, it's one of the highest-rated MCU films.
  • Rotten Tomatoes: Critics gave it 90% while audiences rated it 98%, showing widespread acclaim.
  • Metacritic: A score of 71 reflects strong critical appreciation for the film's storytelling.
  • Letterboxd: Fans loved it with a 4.1/5 average rating across hundreds of thousands of reviews.
  • Box Office Success: The film became a global phenomenon, breaking records worldwide.
