Story

Superman (2025) is a reboot of the Superman film series and the first entry in James Gunn’s new DC Universe. The film follows Clark Kent as he struggles to balance his Kryptonian heritage with his human upbringing. He must prove himself as Earth’s protector while facing Lex Luthor’s schemes and navigating his relationship with Lois Lane. The story emphasizes hope, humanity, and Superman’s role as a symbol of inspiration.

Reviews

  • Letterboxd: Fans are enthusiastic, with nearly 263K ★★★ ratings and a strong average of 3.9/5. It’s one of the most discussed films of 2025 on the platform.
  • IMDb: With a 7.1/10 rating and over 360K votes, it’s both critically and popularly embraced. IMDb even ranked it the most popular movie of 2025.
  • Rotten Tomatoes: Critics and audiences align with an 86–87% score, putting it in the top tier of DC superhero films.
  • Metacritic: While critics are a bit more reserved at 68, the user score matches IMDb at 7.1, showing consistent audience approval.
  • X (Twitter): Social media buzz is highly positive, with fans calling it “near-perfect” and praising Corenswet’s Superman portrayal.
