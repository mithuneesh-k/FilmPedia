Story

Nanban (2012) is the Tamil remake of *3 Idiots*. It follows three engineering students—Pari, Venkat, and Senthil—through their journey of friendship, struggles, and self-discovery. Pari inspires his friends to pursue their passions rather than conforming to societal pressures. The film blends humor, emotion, and life lessons, emphasizing creativity and individuality over rote learning.

Fun Facts

  • Nanban is a Tamil remake of the Hindi blockbuster *3 Idiots* (2009).
  • Director Shankar retained much of the original’s humor and emotional depth while adding Tamil cultural touches.
  • Vijay’s performance as Pari was widely praised for its charm and energy.
  • The film’s soundtrack by Harris Jayaraj became a chartbuster in Tamil Nadu.
  • Nanban was released during Pongal festival and became a major box office success.
