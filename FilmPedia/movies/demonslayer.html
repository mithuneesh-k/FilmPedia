Story

Following the Hashira Training, the Demon Slayers find themselves suddenly pulled into Muzan Kibutsuji’s infinite, ever-changing lair. This first film of the trilogy captures the intense, high-stakes battles between the Hashira and the strongest Upper Rank demons. It focuses on the themes of vengeance, sacrifice, and the unbreakable bond of the Demon Slayer Corps as they fight to end the era of demons once and for all.

Reviews

  • Letterboxd: Fans have rated it an incredible 4.6/5, citing it as the peak of modern animation quality.
  • IMDb: Debuted with a 9.2/10, making it one of the highest-rated anime films of all time.
  • Rotten Tomatoes: Currently holding a 98% Fresh rating from critics and a 99% Audience Score.
  • Animation Quality: Critics are calling the 3D environmental work of the "Infinity Castle" a milestone in digital cinema.

Watcher's Guide

  • Release Date: July 18, 2025 (Global Release)
  • Runtime: 135 minutes
  • Format: Strictly recommended for IMAX to appreciate the "Infinite" scale of the castle.
  • Trilogy Info: This is Movie 1 of 3. Ensure you have watched the Hashira Training Arc before viewing.

Fun Facts

  • Ufotable reportedly spent over 4 years in pre-production for the castle’s layout.
  • This is the first time a major Shonen manga finale is being adapted as a theatrical trilogy.
  • The soundtrack features a 100-piece orchestra to heighten the cinematic tension.
  • The film broke opening weekend records in Japan, surpassing Mugen Train's initial numbers.
