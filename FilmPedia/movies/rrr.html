Story

Set in 1920s British-occupied India, the story follows Bheem, a tribal protector on a mission to rescue a kidnapped girl, and Raju, a high-ranking police officer serving the British. Though they become best friends unknowingly, their conflicting loyalties eventually lead to an explosive confrontation. The film is a masterclass in "maximalist" cinema, blending high-octane action with a deep emotional core about brotherhood and revolution.

Reviews

  • Rotten Tomatoes: 95% Critics Score. Critics worldwide called it "unbelievably entertaining" and "a triumph of imagination."
  • Letterboxd: 4.2/5. It became a viral sensation in the West, leading to sold-out "Encore" screenings across the US.
  • Oscars: Made history by winning the **Academy Award for Best Original Song** (Naatu Naatu).
  • Global Impact: Ranked as one of the best films of 2022 by the National Board of Review.

Watcher's Guide

  • Runtime: 187 minutes (3 hours 7 mins).
  • Budget: ₹550 crore ($72 million), making it one of India's most expensive films.
  • Box Office: Over ₹1,300 crore ($175 million) worldwide.
  • Best Experience: Must be watched on the biggest screen possible to appreciate the scale of the VFX and action.

Fun Facts

  • The "Naatu Naatu" song was filmed in Ukraine at the Mariinskyi Palace (the Presidential Palace) just months before the war began.
  • The film's title RRR stands for **Rise, Roar, Revolt** (originally a working title based on the names of the Director and lead actors).
  • The interval fight sequence took 65 nights to film and involved thousands of background actors.
  • Director S.S. Rajamouli spent 300 days just on principal photography.
  • The bridge rescue scene alone took years of pre-visualization and months of filming to perfect.
