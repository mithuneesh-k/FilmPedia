Story

Serendipity (2001) tells the story of Jonathan and Sara, who meet by chance in New York City. Believing in destiny, Sara insists they let fate decide if they are meant to be together. Years later, both are engaged to other people but still feel drawn to each other, leading to a romantic search across time and distance.

Fun Facts

  • The film’s title comes from the famous Serendipity 3 dessert café in New York City.
  • John Cusack and Kate Beckinsale had instant chemistry, which made the film memorable.
  • The movie has become a popular holiday romance, often rewatched during Christmas season.
  • Marc Klein, the writer, based the story on his own fascination with fate and chance encounters.
  • The café scenes were filmed at the real Serendipity 3, a landmark in NYC.
